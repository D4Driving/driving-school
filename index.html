Common Questions

Section 1 Getting Started (Na start)

Do I need to pass my Theory Test before starting lessons? ▼

No, you don't! You can start your practical driving lessons as soon as you have your Provisional Driving License. In fact, learning the theory while driving often helps you understand the rules of the road faster. However, you will need to pass your Theory Test before booking your final Practical Driving Test.

Which areas of Peterborough do you cover? ▼

I cover most of Peterborough and surrounding areas, including Hampton, Orton, Werrington, Gunthorpe, Fengate and more. If you live further out, please contact me before booking to ensure I can accommodate your location.

Do you offer Manual or Automatic lessons? ▼

I offer both! You can choose to learn in a manual Toyota Aygo X (great for beginners) or an automatic Toyota Yaris Cross (perfect for stress-free driving). Both cars are modern, dual-controlled, and air-conditioned for your comfort.

Do you speak other languages? / Czy mówisz po polsku? ▼

Yes! I am a fluent English and Polish speaker. If you prefer to have your driving lessons explained in Polish to better understand technical details, I am happy to accommodate that.

Tak, prowadzę lekcje w języku angielskim i polskim.

Section 2 Booking & Payments (Płatności)

How do I book my first lesson? ▼

The fastest way is to use the "Book Online" section on this app. You’ll need your Provisional License number and pickup address. Please note:

  • Choose "Manual" or "Automatic".
  • The first lesson must be prepaid to secure your slot.
  • I recommend booking a 1.5-hour slot for your first session so we can properly assess your skills.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼

I accept Cash, Bank Transfer, and Credit/Debit Cards.
Pay-as-you-go: Payment must be made no later than 48 hours before the lesson.
Block Bookings: 10, 15, or 20-hour blocks must be paid upfront (this guarantees your price and slots).

Section 3 Cancellations & Anxiety (Zasady)

What is your cancellation policy? ▼

I understand that plans change. However, to protect my schedule and other students waiting for slots, I require at least 48 hours' notice to cancel or reschedule a lesson without charge. Cancellations made within 48 hours will be charged in full.

I’m very nervous about driving. Can you help? ▼

Absolutely. Specializing in "Anxiety Busting" is a core part of D4Driving. I use a patient, calm approach and we can start on quiet roads (like industrial estates in Fengate) until you feel confident. Many of my students started out terrified and passed with flying colours!

Do you provide the car for the driving test? ▼

Yes, provided you have reached the required test standard. We will use the same Toyota you learned in (Manual or Auto) for your test, so you feel completely comfortable on the big day. The test day booking usually includes a 1-hour warm-up drive beforehand and 1 hour for the test (a 2 hr slot).
